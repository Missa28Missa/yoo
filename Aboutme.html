Missa, as an artist?

Well, I love art. For me art is life, love and peace. If you ask me if I would like to be an artist in the future, I will answer you "Yes, wh not?". I really like to draw and create new things, for example: I like to draw about how I feel, things I like, I like to produce videos with effects and so many things. I consider myself s an artist & I know that inside me is someone that hasn´t showed to the world his art.
Imagine, I grew dreaming to be an astronaut. Then I started to study and I learned a lot of things while I was growing, now I see that if I want to be an astronaut I have to study a carreer and that is not free, I have to pay a big amount of money for it. I cannot pay that money, so I have to decide what to do. If I love art, and I´m good on it.. Why not?
I´m still growing and I don´t know what will happen in the next five, seven, ten years. I´ll try to make my dreams come true, it will be difficult, but not impossible. I also want to be a famous guy on internet, I see myself making streams of me doing whatever I want. That´s my biggest dream (in terms of earning money doing what I love the most). So, I´m still thinking about what to do in this time.
